# 🎥 Accessible Video Recorder for Visually Impaired Creators
#by SeeSharp under the Category of Visual Impairment

An inclusive web application that enables visually impaired users to independently record and create short-form videos for platforms like TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts.

The system removes the need for visual interaction by relying entirely on voice commands and audio feedback.

## 🌟 Key Features

### 🎙️ Voice-Controlled Interaction
Control the entire app using simple voice commands:
- Start recording  
- Stop recording  
- Play  
- Cut  
- Export  

No buttons or manual navigation required.

---

### 🎯 Real-Time Camera Guidance
The app provides spoken instructions to help you stay centered:
- “Move left to centre yourself”  
- “Move right”  
- “You are centred. Great position.”  

This ensures proper framing without needing to see the screen.

---

### 🎬 Easy Video Recording
- Record clips up to **2 minutes long**  
- Clips are automatically saved with names and durations  
- Audio feedback confirms every action  

---

### ♿ Accessibility-First Design
- Fully usable without sight  
- Minimal and uncluttered interface  
- Clear spoken guidance throughout  

---



## 🚀 Purpose and Impact

This project is designed specifically for visually impaired users.

It aims to:
- Enable independent video creation  
- Remove reliance on visual navigation  
- Provide real-time spoken assistance for framing and recording  

---

## ▶️ How to Use

1. Open the app in your browser  
2. Allow access to your **camera** and **microphone**  
3. Follow the spoken instructions to position yourself  
4. Use voice commands to record and edit clips  
5. Export your final video and share it online  

---

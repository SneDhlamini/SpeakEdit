🎬 SpeakToEdit

Hands-free video editing. Control everything with your voice.
Here's what you can say:

start recordingBegin capture
stop recordingSave clip
playPlay selected clip
pausePause playback
cutSplit at playhead
delete clipRemove selected
select clip 1Pick a clip
next / previous clipNavigate clips
undoReverse last action
add songAdd music to clip
remove songRemove music
exportDownload clips
helpHear all commands

The app will read the commands aloud when you start.
